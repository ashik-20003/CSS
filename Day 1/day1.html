Lenovo LOQ Gaming Laptop

Price - 1,00,000/-

Lenovo LOQ (pronounced "lock") is a line of budget-friendly, high-performance gaming laptops and towers designed as an entry-level alternative to the premium Legion series. Targeting new gamers, these devices feature AI-tuned performance (LA1 chip), NVIDIA GeForce RTX graphics, Intel Core or AMD Ryzen processors, and advanced cooling systems. They are available in 15-inch and 17-inch sizes with high-refresh-rate displays.
